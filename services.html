What We Offer


At FazzTracks we offer bike rentals, guided tours, and skills clinics for riders of all levels. We also provide trail maps, repair services, and a gear shop. Shuttle services, bike wash facilities, and community events are also available, creating a hub for mountain biking enthusiasts.

Price List


Bike Rentals: Basic mountain bike: £20-£40 per day Premium or specialty bikes: £40-£80 per day


Guided Tours: Half-day tour: £40-£60 Full-day tour: £80-£120


Gear and Equipment Shop: Prices vary based on the type of gear, starting from £10 for accessories and going up for specialized equipment.


Shuttle Services: Shuttle to trailhead: £10-£20 one way Shuttle packages for multiple rides: £30-£50


Bike Wash Facilities: Typically included with rental or service, otherwise £5-£10 per wash


Community Events: Varies, with some events being free or having a nominal entry fee


Café or Lounge Area: Prices for food and beverages vary based on the menu, typically starting at £5.

What To Expect On Arrival


Arriving at the center, you'll be greeted with diverse bike rentals, guided tours, and skills clinics. Trail maps, a gear shop, and convenient amenities like shuttles and bike repairs are available. The welcoming atmosphere extends to a cafe or lounge, creating a hub for all levels of riders to enjoy their mountain biking experience.
